<p><p>The aviation electric motors market includes several types categorized by their construction and operational characteristics. DC Brush Motors offer simplicity and robustness but need regular maintenance due to brush wear. DC Brush Torque Motors provide high torque at low speeds, suitable for precise control. DC Brushless Torque Motors combine the benefits of brushless technology with high torque capabilities. AC Induction Motors are reliable and widely used for various aviation functions, offering durability and efficiency.</p></p>
