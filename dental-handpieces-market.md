<p><ul><li>Air-Driven Handpiece</li><li>Electric Handpiece</li><li>Hybrid Air-Electric Handpiece</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental handpieces market features three main types: air-driven, electric, and hybrid air-electric handpieces. Air-driven handpieces use compressed air to operate, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, making them widely used in dental clinics. Electric handpieces provide consistent torque and speed, enhancing precision and reducing operator fatigue but come at a higher cost. Hybrid air-electric handpieces combine both technologies, allowing for versatility and improved performance in various dental procedures, catering to a range of clinician preferences and patient needs.</p></p>
