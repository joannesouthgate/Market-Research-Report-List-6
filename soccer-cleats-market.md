<p><p>The soccer cleats market is segmented into several types based on playing surfaces. FG (firm ground) cleats feature conical studs for use on natural grass fields. SG (soft ground) cleats offer longer, replaceable studs suited for wet, muddy conditions. HG (hard ground) cleats have shorter, more numerous studs for hard, dry pitches. TF (turf) cleats provide a flatter sole with rubber studs for artificial turf surfaces. AG (artificial ground) cleats are designed for synthetic pitches, combining features for optimal traction and comfort.</p></p>
