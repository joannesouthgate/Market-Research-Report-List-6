<p><p>Automotive Pedestrian Protection Systems (PPS) are designed to enhance pedestrian safety during vehicle collisions. They are categorized into two main types: Passive Pedestrian Protection Systems, which incorporate design elements like crumple zones, hoods, and bumpers to reduce injury severity in impacts; and Active Pedestrian Protection Systems, which use technologies such as sensors and cameras to detect pedestrians and automatically activate braking or other safety measures to avoid collisions.
