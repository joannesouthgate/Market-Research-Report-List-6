<p><strong>The Hydrogen Generation by Water Electrolysis Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Alkaline Water Electrolysis</li><li>Proton Exchange membrane (PEM) Electrolysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydrogen generation market via water electrolysis primarily consists of two types: Alkaline Water Electrolysis and Proton Exchange Membrane (PEM) Electrolysis. Alkaline water electrolysis utilizes an alkaline electrolyte, typically potassium hydroxide, leading to lower costs and long-term stability but lower efficiency. Conversely, PEM electrolysis employs a solid polymer membrane as an electrolyte, offering higher efficiency and faster response times, making it ideal for dynamic applications. Both technologies play crucial roles in advancing sustainable hydrogen production.</p></p>
