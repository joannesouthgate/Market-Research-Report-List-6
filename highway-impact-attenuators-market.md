The market can be divided into two main types: non-redirective impact attenuators, which are designed to absorb energy and reduce damage without redirecting the vehicle, and redirective impact attenuators, which not only absorb impact but also guide the vehicle back onto the road, minimizing the risk of secondary collisions. Both types are crucial for enhancing road safety and protecting passengers and infrastructure.</p></p>
