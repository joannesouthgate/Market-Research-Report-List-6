<p><strong>The Veterinary Thermography Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short-wavelength Thermography Cameras</li><li>Mid-wavelength Thermography Cameras</li><li>Long-wavelength Thermography Cameras</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The veterinary thermography market is categorized into three types based on the wavelength of thermal radiation detected. Short-wavelength thermography cameras primarily capture temperatures in the near-infrared range, useful for surface observations. Mid-wavelength cameras detect moderate infrared radiation, suitable for assessing core body temperatures in animals. Long-wavelength thermography cameras focus on far-infrared radiation, ideal for deeper tissue analysis and evaluating inflammation. Each type serves distinct applications in veterinary diagnostics, treatment monitoring, and overall animal health assessments.</p></p>
