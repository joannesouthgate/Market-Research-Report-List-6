<p><ul><li>LCOS based Wavelength Selective Switch (WSS) Modules</li><li>MEMS based Wavelength Selective Switch (WSS) Modules</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wavelength Selective Switch (WSS) modules are essential in optical networks for routing multiple wavelengths effectively. LCOS (Liquid Crystal on Silicon) based WSS modules utilize liquid crystal technology for precise wavelength manipulation, offering high scalability and flexibility. In contrast, MEMS (Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems) based WSS modules utilize micro-mirrors for fast and reliable switching. Both types enhance network efficiency but differ in performance metrics, applications, and cost structures, catering to diverse market needs in telecommunications and data transmission.</p></p>
