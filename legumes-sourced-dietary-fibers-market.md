<p><p>The legumes-sourced dietary fibers market is primarily divided into soluble and insoluble dietary fibers. Soluble dietary fiber, found in legumes like beans and lentils, dissolves in water and helps regulate blood sugar and cholesterol levels. It promotes a healthy gut by facilitating digestion. Insoluble dietary fiber, on the other hand, does not dissolve in water; it adds bulk to stool, aiding regular bowel movements and preventing constipation. Both types are crucial for a balanced diet and overall health.</p></p>
