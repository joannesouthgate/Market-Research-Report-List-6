<p><p>A cautery pencil, also known as an electrocautery or diathermy pen, is a medical instrument used for cutting tissue and coagulating blood vessels during surgical procedures. The technology is widely employed in various surgical disciplines, including dermatology, gynecology, and general surgery.</p><p>The Cautery Pencil Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10% during the forecast period.
