<p><p>The medical imaging instrumentation market comprises several key types. X-ray systems are widely used for diagnosing fractures and dental issues. Ultrasound devices utilize sound waves for real-time imaging, particularly during pregnancy. MRI employs magnetic fields for detailed internal imaging, and computed tomography (CT) combines X-ray data for cross-sectional views of organs. Nuclear imaging uses radioactive substances to visualize metabolic processes. Additionally, the "Others" category includes advanced technologies like PET scans and optical imaging systems, enriching diagnostic capabilities.</p></p>
