<p><p>The copper-based wood preservative market primarily includes three key types: Alkaline Copper Quaternary (ACQ), Micronized Copper Azole (MCA), and Copper Azole (CA). ACQ combines copper and a quaternary ammonium compound to enhance wood protection against decay and insects, while MCA utilizes micronized copper particles for improved penetration and effectiveness. Copper Azole employs copper in tandem with azole compounds to offer similar protective benefits. These formulations are widely used for outdoor applications due to their durability and environmental acceptance.</p></p>
