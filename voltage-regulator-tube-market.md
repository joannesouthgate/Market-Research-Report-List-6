<p><ul><li>Ferroresonant</li><li>Tap Switching</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The voltage-regulator tube market includes two primary types: ferroresonant and tap switching. Ferroresonant regulators utilize magnetic fields and resonance effects to maintain steady voltage levels across varying loads, offering high reliability and minimal maintenance. Tap switching regulators, on the other hand, adjust output voltage by switching between preset tap points on a transformer, allowing for more flexibility in managing fluctuating electrical demands. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring stable power supply in diverse applications, from industrial to residential settings.</p></p>
