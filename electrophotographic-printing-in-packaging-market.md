<p><p>Electrophotographic printing in the packaging market encompasses various types based on the ink and curing methods used. Aqueous printing utilizes water-based inks, offering vibrant colors and eco-friendliness. Latex printing employs water-based latex inks, focusing on durability and flexibility for diverse packaging materials. Solvent printing uses solvent-based inks, ensuring strong adhesion and weather resistance for outdoor applications. UV-cured printing utilizes ultraviolet light to instantly cure inks, providing high-quality images and quick turnaround times, ideal for high-volume packaging needs.</p></p>
