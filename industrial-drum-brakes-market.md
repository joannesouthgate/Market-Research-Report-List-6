<p><p>Industrial drum brakes are essential for various applications and come in three main types: simplex, duplex, and servo. Simplex brakes utilize a single shoe for braking and are ideal for lighter loads. Duplex brakes feature two shoes for enhanced stopping power, making them suitable for medium to heavy applications. Servo brakes, offering self-energizing capabilities, enhance braking efficiency and are commonly used in heavy-duty environments. Each type is designed to meet specific load and performance requirements, ensuring safety and reliability.</p></p>
